    英语作文万能句型

    #英语/作文

    现象描述

    1. In a time of constant change and stiff competition, challenges are everywhere, ranging from taking a test to seeking a job. 在瞬息万变、竞争激烈的今天, 从应试到求职, 挑战可谓无处不在.
    2. … has attracted extensive attention in the society, which can be found in TV programs, newspapers, university classes, and many aspects of our life. ……已经引起了社会的广泛关注, 可以在电视节目、报纸、大学课堂和我们生活的许多方面找到.
    3. The following facts, however, are indisputable. 然而, 以下是不争的事实.
    4. But the problem is not limited exclusively to the developing world. 这个问题并不仅仅局限于发展中国家.
    5. In recent years, … is becoming increasingly prevalent at an amazing rate. 近年来, … 越来越流行.
    6. And the problem is growing. 而且问题越来越严重

    分析原因

    1. What are the reasons for it? In the following sentences, I’ll venture to explore the reasons. To start with, … In addition,… 原因是什么? 在下面的句子中, 我将大胆地探讨原因. 首先, …… 另外, ……
    2. What accounts for this stark contrast? 怎样解释这种鲜明的对比呢？
    3. A number of factors might contribute to this situation. One of the most common factors is …许多因素可能导致这种情况. 最常见的因素之一是……
    4. Perhaps the primary reason is … 也许最主要的原因是……
    5. But the fundamental element is …但最基本的因素是……
    6. This is largely due to … 这在很大程度上是因为……

    引语解释

    1. In this globalized era, we need to remind ourselves of that cliched yet compelling saying: wealth of the minds is the only true wealth. 在这个全球化的时代, 我们需要提醒自己那句陈词滥调却令人信服的话:思想的财富是唯一真正的财富.
    2. Why this saying makes sense is simple: … 这句话的意义很简单: ……
    3. But as the saying goes: From confrontation comes brilliance. 但俗话说:对抗才有才华.
    4. Just as the saying goes, what is worth doing is worth doing well. 正如俗话所说, 值得做的事就值得做好.
    5. “Science and everyday life cannot and should not be separated.” Those words were uttered by one of the pioneering British scientists who firmly believed that the pursuit of science should be accessible to all. “科学和日常生活不能也不应该分开. ”这句话出自英国先驱科学家罗莎琳德·富兰克林之口, 她坚信所有人都应该追求科学.

    强调

    1. But in fact, it means much more than that. 但事实上, 它的意义远不止于此.
    2. we … cannot emphasize the importance of … too much. 再怎么强调…的重要性也不为过.
    3. We cannot emphasize the importance of cultivating the ability to meet challenges for us students. 对我们学生来说, 再怎么强调培养应对挑战的能力也不为过.

    举例子

    1. Abounds in examples that demonstrate this point. 有很多的事例都能够证明这一点.

    观点结论

    1. From what has been discussed above, we may safely draw the conclusion that … 综上所述, 我们可以得出结论…
    2. It is difficult to say whether… is good or not in general as it depends very much on the sit-uation of…. However, from a personal point of view… 我们很难说是好还是坏, 因为它取决于…. 然而, 在我看来…
    3. As I see it,.. indeed of great importance as they not only help to … but also contribute to more … Therefore, I am in favor of … 在我看来, ……确实很重要, 因为它不仅帮助我们…还帮助我们……·因此, 我赞成……

    漫画描述

    1. Let’s take a close look at the drawing, … 让我们仔细看看这幅画, ……
    2. Undoubtedly, the author’s real purpose is not the drawing itself, but to lead us to secure what hides behind the picture. 毫无疑问, 作者的真正目的不是图画本身, 而是引导我们去了解图画背后的东西.
    3. How impressive the cartoon is in describing the most widespread social phenomenon concerning … 这幅漫画描述了关于…的最普遍的社会现象, 令人印象深刻.
    4. The line graph shows that the past two decades have seen great progress in education, reflecting the phenomenon that … 曲线图显示在过去的二十年里教育取得了巨大的进步, 反映了……的现象
