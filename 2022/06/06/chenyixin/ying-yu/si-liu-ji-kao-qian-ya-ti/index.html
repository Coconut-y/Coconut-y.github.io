Undoubtedly, the author’s real purpose is not the drawing itself, but to lead us to secure what hides behind the picture. What are the reasons for it? In the following sentences, I’ll venture to explore the reasons.

To start with, spelling mistakes will result in unnecessary misunderstandings in written communication. In addition, an unintentional spelling error could cause serious consequences on some formal occasions. Abounds in examples that demonstrate this point. As I see, the best case in point is if you make a spelling mistake in the name of your client, you might face a great loss of money.

The author’s real purpose is not the drawing itself, but to lead us to secure what hides behind the picture.

What are the reasons for it? In the following sentences, I’ll venture to explore it.

To start with, experience is usually what employers value because an experienced employee means higher efficiency, better profits, and less cost of training.
In addition, those with new perspectives are more likely to drive innovation, which can yield an incredible return.
Abounds in examples that demonstrate this point.

In a nutshell, we cannot emphasize the importance of both experience and fresh perspective too much. As a university student, not only do I consider having rich experiences an advantageous aspect for young people, but I also deem it imperative to have some fresh perspectives.

02 信息爆炸

Directions: For this part, you are allowed 30 minutes to write an essay about the impact of the information explosion by referring to the saying “A wealth of information creates a poverty of attention.” You can give examples to illustrate your point and then explain what you can do to avoid being distracted by irrelevant information. You should write at least 150 words but no more than 200 words.

The Impact of the Information Explosion

As the saying goes, “A wealth of information creates a poverty of attention.”. It is indicated in the remark that the importance of getting over information explosion can never be underestimated.

In recent years, the information explosion has attracted extensive attention in society, which can be found in TV programs, newspapers, university classes and many aspects of our life. It is generally agreed that a lot of information has been playing an increasingly important role in our life, which has brought us a lot of benefits but has triggered some enormous problems as well.

Abounds in examples that demonstrate this point. As I see, the best case in point is when searching for something online, people are easily misguided by irrelevant information and forget their original plans. And here comes one relevant suggestion, which is to make a list of what you really want before your search, hopefully, this may be helpful to you.

In a nutshell, we must keep highly alter of the problems evoked by it, because once the harmful influence accumulates to a certain degree, it will surely threaten the development of the whole society.
